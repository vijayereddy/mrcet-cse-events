INTO THE GOVERNMENT CIPHER AND CODE SCHOOL: -

ENIGMA TRAINING: -
The selected teams will be trained in the elite GC&CS.

Prove your mettle by conveying top secret messages to your team members by decrypting and encrypting them in specific codes before the other teams.

4. Judges and co-coordinators’ decision is final and all teams shall abide by it.
5. Briefing for each round will be given before the commencement of rounds.

 Q. Who all can participate?

 A. Anyone

 Q.Is there any special requirement for the event?

 A. Nothing special, you just have to come in teams of three.

 Q.Is it necessary to come in a team of three?

 A. Yes, you have to come in teams of three only. In case of shortage of members in a team we’ll try to come up with  appropriate solution.

  Q. How to register for the event?

  Online registration on  <www.technozion.org> OR

  You can also register yourself at the site of the event (onsiteregistrations).

Q.  Is there any payment for participation in the event?

A. No, it is absolutely free. Participate, clear the rounds and go backwith lots of prizes!

Q. Do I need to bring a laptop?

 A. No need to bring laptop. Just come with a pen.
