DETAILS

PROBLEM STATEMENT :

Design and make a wireless remote controlled aircraft, which has all three degrees of freedom i.e. along X, Y and Z axis and that can complete a specified path in the least specified time.

ROUND DETAILS:

Event consists of two rounds.

Round 1(preferably on day one): Aircraft is supposed to complete the arena and land safely in the landing zone. People who qualify in the 1st round will be selected for second round.

Round 2(preferably on day two): In this round participants are given freedom to choose their own manoeuver which are awarded with different points depending on their complexity.

There are some manoeuvers available for the participants.
The timer will start from the moment the aircraft is in the air.

ARENA :

ROUND 1: 

Aircraft is supposed to complete the arena and land safely in the landing zone

PATH SPECIFICATIONS-

  • Participants will have to hand-launch their aircraft from within the launching zone.
  • The number of poles will be 3 or 4 (will be decided by judge).
  • The aircraft should be launched in the direction shown in the figures.
  • The launching and landing zones would be circles of 20 meter radii.
  • Distance between pole 1 and pole 2 is 35 meters

  • The path will be decided by the judge and will be informed to all the teams beforehand

MACHINE SPECIFICATIONS-

 An aircraft is defined as an object that has the four forces of flight, namely lift, drag, weight (gravity) and thrust.

  1. The wing span of the aircraft should be anything less than or equal to 60 inches.
  2. The voltage of the battery should not exceed 12 V or that of 3 cell Li-Po battery or equivalent
  3. The weight of the aircraft should be minimum as judging will be done based on the weight also
  4. The use of launching mechanisms is prohibited. The aircraft should be hand launched.
  5. The use of IC engines is prohibited. Only electrical motors are allowed.
  6. The participants are free to use the materials of their choice. However, the use of Balsa wood or foam (sun board) or sun pack is advisable. Balsa wood is light, easy to handle and fabricate the Aircraft making it the best choice.
  7. Participants must make all parts of the aircraft themselves. Usage of Ready-to-Fly (RTF) and Almost-Ready-to-Fly (ARF) kits is strictly prohibited. However, the kit comprising of unassembled cut-pieces of Balsa wood is allowed. Also use of readymade actuators/motors, remote controls and propellers is allowed.

Use of gyroscopes (gyros) is prohibited.

If anyone found not following above rules will be disqualified.

Competition rules:

  • Participant has to hand launch the aircraft from the launching zone and has to complete the path as specified in fig: and has to land inside the circle (points will be awarded for landing inside the circle) as shown.
  • The participants will be given 2 runs per round to perform these tasks.
  • The participants will be allowed a total of 3 chances to launch the aircraft in every run.
  • A maximum time of 3 minutes per run will be given to perform the move.

  • The use of RTF (ready-to-fly) and ARF (almost-ready-to-fly) kits are strictly prohibited.

  • The organizers reserve all rights to change any or all of the above rules as they deem fit. Change in any rule, if any, will be highlighted on the website.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding. No correspondence will be entertained in this regard.
  • The rounds are conducted in given specified time, if any team could not make in time inform beforehand. We request you to be on time .so rounds can be conducted in front of all the teams. no disputes occur further.

ARENA:

  • The whole championship arena is divided into various stages of evaluation.
  • The flying of quadcopter will be from the specific Source to Destination.
  • There are specific check points where the teams will be given relaxation and the teams can make use of the checkpoints for changing the batteries, adjusting gains, calibration of sensors, etc.
  • There will be enough time provided for the teams to charge the batteries at the completion of every stage. The requirement of time of charging the batteries during the conduct of competition will not be entertained.
  • The teams can skip the stages in which they think it is not possible for them to complete.

 

STAGE 1 :

LADDER CROSSING

  • This stage meets the perfection in the stability of the quadcopter.
  • During this stage the quadcopter has to travel through the gaps in the huge sized ladder.

STAGE 2:

OBSTACLE COURSE

  • An obstacle arena will be constructed using rings, walls, tunnels etc.,
  • The teams must go through the designed course without coming in contact with any of the obstacles.
  • Points will be awarded based on the time taken for the completion of the course. 
  • The faster they complete the course, the higher they score.
  • The whole course has a time limit of 5 minutes.
  • Every team starts out with 600 points, when the timer starts their points start decreasing as the time increases. For every second two points will be reduce.

STAGE 3:

TIE BREAKER

  • In case any tie occurring in between the teams during the overall evaluation then the Formula racing of the respective teams will be given.
  • The task is like a knock out race.
  • The team crossing the finish line at first will be awarded winner and the second with the next corresponding position.
