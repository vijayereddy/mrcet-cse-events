PAPER PRESENTATION 

About the event: 

                   To truly achieve one's potential one needs a stage that draws out their inner Edison. is just that stage. Since its inception  has continued its legacy of providing ample opportunity to enhance the scientific culture among the student fraternity and help them nurture their talents. Here is an amazing opportunity to showcase your ideas on a unique and massive platform in the form of well-integrated and intriguing presentation. It strives to explore the inquisitiveness of young minds by giving their incredulous ideas a definitive direction.  2019 brings you the event Paper Presentation that provides you an extravagant platform to showcase and share your technical expertise and master your discipline.

The deadline for abstract submission has been extended to 1st November, 11:59 pm and now Spot Registrations for Paper Presentation is also available.

  • The interested candidates are invited to send their abstracts with the title of the chosen topic for presentation as the subject of the mail along with their contact details to the mail address corresponding to the branch of the chosen topic before the deadline date i.e. 1st November 2019, 11:59 pm or you can register on the day of the event.
  • The sample topics for each branch and the mail addresses for the respective branches for sending the abstracts are mentioned in the tabs designed for individual branches (the tab names for different branches are their branch codes respectively).
  • The shortlisted candidates would be informed personally either through phone/email and will have to present their paper during  in front of a panel of judges.
  • The shortlisted candidates will have to register for event participation on 2019 website(www..org).

RULES AND REGULATIONS:  

  • The paper should strictly follow standard IEEE two-column format (The format can be downloaded from the internet).
  • The paper should be in PDF format, readable in Adobe acrobat reader.
  • A team should consist of a maximum of 3 members.
  • Paper should contain maximum of EIGHT pages.
  • Citing the references is MANDATORY
  • Presentation should be less than FIVE minutes.
  • Qualified candidates are required to bring along with them one soft copy of a Powerpoint presentation(preferably in a pen drive) and 3 hard copies of the technical paper on the day of the event.
  • The decision of the judges and event head shall be treated as final and binding on all.
  • Participants will be evaluated based on their:
  1. Subject knowledge & background research and originality in presentation
  2. Confidence while presenting, presentability and preparedness
  3. Spontaneity and aptness during questionnaire
  4. Organisation skills and effective utilisation of time

NOTE: The participants need not restrict themselves to the topics mentioned in the respective branch tabs.
